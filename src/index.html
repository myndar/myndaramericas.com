About Myndar

Architects & Visionaries of Innovative Technology Solutions for Specialized Retailers

Founded in Hong Kong in 2009, Myndar is responsible for the research and development of technology solutions designed specifically for the jewelry and timepiece industries to streamline business, privide greater efficiencies, profitablity, higher security and profound business insights.

Through various Myndar retail tools that house cutting-edge RFID "smart tag" technology, retailer are afforded visibility on inventory, security on high value items, insights on store associate selling behavior as well as customer purchasing behaviors.

About RFID

RFID is short for Radio Frequency IDentification, also known as "smart tag" technology.

In industries like fashion and apparel, RFID "smart tag" technology is rapidly replacing the traditional UPC bar code as the go-to tagging and tracking tool of choice.

Unlike bar codes, RFID "smart tags" allow companies to electronically store product data on tags resulting in a number of benefits:

  • Increased inventory accuracy
  • Increased visibility of stock
  • Greater staff productivity
  • Descreased out-of-stock items
  • Decreased shrinkage
  • Greater customer satisfaction
  • Decreased markdowns
  • Increased profit margins
