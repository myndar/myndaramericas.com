RFID solutions designed for the jewelry industry.

About Myndar

Architects & Visionaries of Innovative Technology Solutions for Specialized Retailers

Founded in Hong Kong in 2009, Myndar is responsible for the research and development of technology solutions designed specifically for the jewelry and timepiece industries to streamline business; provide greater efficiencies; profitability; higher security and profound business insights.

Beginning in early 2017, a partnership with Pac Team Group established Myndar Americas, offering the same advanced retail technology solutions to the North and South American retail luxury industries under Pac Team’s PT Next technology division. Through various Myndar retail tools that house cutting-edge RFID “smart tag” technology, retailers are afforded visibility on inventory, security on high value items as well as insights on story associate selling behaviors and customer purchasing behaviors.

About RFID

RFID is short for Radio Frequency IDentification, also known as "smart tag" technology.

In industries like fashion and apparel, RFID "smart tag" technology is rapidly replacing the traditional UPC bar code as the go-to tagging and tracking tool of choice.

Unlike bar codes, RFID "smart tags" allow companies to electronically store product data on tags resulting in a number of benefits:

  • Increased inventory accuracy
  • Increased visibility of stock
  • Greater staff productivity
  • Descreased out-of-stock items
  • Decreased shrinkage
  • Greater customer satisfaction
  • Decreased markdowns
  • Increased profit margins

RFID lets your products speak to you.

Imagine knowing how often a product was removed from a case, only to be put back again. Imagine that a novice salesperson instantly knows the best-selling points for every single product in your store. Imagine tracking your product’s movement at all times - who accessed it and when.
